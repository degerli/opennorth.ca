For Better, More Open Democracy

We are an active member of a global community working in favour of inclusive and innovative ecosystems, improving government transparency and accountability, and increasing public participation in democracy towards enhanced citizen and community well-being.

Guided by our core values and strong track record in applied research, we enhance data standards, by cultivating multi-stakeholder partnerships and improving democracy.

We use open data to promote government transparency and accountability while boosting the effectiveness of community social movements and initiatives.
