Open North is looking for an experienced Operations Manager with a strong management background, to grow OpenNorth’s internal accounting and HR operations.

OpenNorth

Founded in 2011, OpenNorth is Canada’s leading not-for-profit organization specialized in open data, open government, community engagement, open smart cities and civic technology. We work with Canada’s largest, most innovative, and connected cities. Our network extends across the country and globally through communities of practice and open government partners. Our work promotes the effective use of data as infrastructure facilitating sustained engagement between government, civil society, and the public. OpenNorth’s online CitizenBudget simulator is used by more than 100 cities across North America and we are currently setting-up a new service advisory team for smart city initiatives initiatives across Canada.

Why Join OpenNorth?

Join a growing and amazing team of people who are passionate about helping improve democracy. With new multi-year program funding confirmed, OpenNorth’s team is growing, and we are looking for a motivated, results-oriented individual to help steer us towards our next stage of maturity. This is an incredibly exciting time for us! Come make a big contribution to increase citizen participation, government transparency and accountability! Join a dynamic, flexible and fun work environment: free coffee, bright sunny office, surrounded by plants.

Job Description

You will be responsible for the efficient running of OpenNorth operations. You will scale new and existing processes to allow the Company to operate effectively and in accordance with upcoming team growth. You will help maintain institutional memory of the organization, by coordinating the accurate recording of financial records, project/programme reporting, and documentation of organization activities, outputs, and policies. You will lead HR activities, including hiring processes and employee evaluation.

Benefits

Free coffee, bright sunny office in the Mile End, flexible schedule, opportunity to shape an organization’s move to its next stage of maturity. Health benefits after successful completion of a 3 month probation period. Paid vacation.

Profile

The successful candidate has demonstrable experience in two areas (both mandatory): accounting and human resources. The candidate will be obsessed with details and making sure things add up. No detail is too small.

The candidate will have an unwavering set of ethics and high personal accountability for a job well done. Also important is a positive, problem-solving attitude and the ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic environment. Strong listening ability, teamwork, and communication skills are highly desirable traits.

Responsibilities

The successful candidate has demonstrable experience in two areas (both mandatory): accounting and human resources.

  • Work with and directly support the Executive Director and senior staff
  • Monitor all internal activities and coordinate reporting requirements, including preparing documentation for quarterly Board of Directors meetings
Operations:
  • Budget planning and forecasting across projects and programmes
  • Grant administration and reporting, including tracking timesheets
  • Communications coordination, including website content updates, in coordination with OpenNorth’s lead developer and communications staff
  • Work with the fundraising strategist to set and achieve OpenNorth's growth strategy
Coordinate logistics across the organization:
  • Coordinate meetings and travel arrangements for the OpenNorth team
  • Coordinate office supplies and equipment needs, including office purchases such as laptops, software licenses
  • Maintain all technical aspects of the organization
Oversee HR processes across the organization:
  • Oversee processing of payroll in an accurate and timely manner, in coordination with OpenNorth’s bookkeeper
  • Maintain employee personnel files
  • Maintain company-wide policies, including insurance and vacation policies
  • Issue contracts
  • Facilitate annual staff evaluations and the development of staff professional development plans in coordination with senior staff
Coordinate financial reporting activities:
  • Oversee processing of accounts payable and receivable and ensure prompt collection of receivables, in coordination with OpenNorth’s part-time bookkeeper
  • Lead monthly budget meetings with senior staff and produce financial documentation for quarterly Board of Directors meetings
  • Process invoices for international and Canadian clients
Coordinate internal documentation activities:
  • Organizational document management practices and archiving, including enf of project and program evaluations.

  • Strong leadership skills (eg. experience working with teams of 10 to 20 employees)
  • Minimum 5 years experience in HR or operations
  • Experience managing yearly budgets of more than $1 mn
  • Experience with Microsoft Office, including MS Excel
  • Experience with G Suite (Google) or equivalent platform
  • Experience with integrating task management software with company processes (experience with project management tool Asana considered an asset)
  • Experience with Quickbooks or similar accounting software
  • Perfectly bilingual French and English
