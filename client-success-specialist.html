Why Join Open North?

Join a growing and amazing team of people who are passionate about helping improve democracy. Come make a big contribution to increase citizen participation, government transparency and accountability!

Open North needs a client success specialist who is passionate about client satisfaction and comfortable with software and technology. You will help the sales team launch customized online budget simulators for governments, school boards, non-profits and others.

Open North’s budget simulator has been used by over 100 federal, provincial, states, and cities.The successful candidate will also support the sales team to find and qualify potential customers.

The successful candidate has superb customer relations experience and strong attention-to-detail. Also important, is a positive, problem-solving attitude and the ability to thrive in a fast-paced, dynamic environment. Strong listening ability, teamwork, and communication skills are highly desirable traits.

Join an awesome dynamic, flexible and fun work environment, with an opportunity to contribute to the growing transparency and online engagement world.

About Open North

Open North is Canada’s leading not-for-profit social enterprise specializing in open data and the development of online tools that promote civic engagement, transparency and accountability. Open North works with governments, media and civil society to connect citizens and decision-makers and to provide citizens with the information and tools they need to track and influence the decision-making processes of government. Within a short period, Open North has established itself as a leader in open government and open data and online budget consultations.

Its highly-innovative and interactive budget consultation tool, is used by municipalities across Canada, the United States and internationally. This open-source, scalable, customizable and highly-effective tool is re-inventing the budget consultation sphere. Citizens enjoy the ease with which they can provide their opinions while cities and other organizations reap valuable feedback and improved stakeholder relationships.
